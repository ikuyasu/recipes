Ingredients:
 - Sticky rice (Korean or Japanese kinds)
 - dried kelp
 - a bit of sake
 - a bit of natural salt

Directions:
 1. Brown the ground beef in a large bot and drain off the excess fat.
 2. Add the tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and cayenne.
 3. Stir, then cover and simmer over low heat for one hour.
 4. Mix the corn flour and 1/2 cup water in a small bowl.  Add to chili.
 5. Add the beans and simmer for 10 more minutes.

 1. Get a Japanese rice cooker, i.e. Zojirushi kind.
 2. In a bowl, wash a cup of rice (serves two people) by swashing
   by water. Drain water and repeat it 3-4 times.
 3. Add a piece of dried kelp and a bit of sake and a grain of salt
  in a pot of rice cooker with washed rice.
 4. Set the bowl up in the rice cook and turn it on.
